About Zoo Boom

Game Overview

Zoo Boom is an addictive animal-matching puzzle game that challenges your quick thinking and strategic skills. Connect colorful animals to create magical combinations and progress through increasingly challenging levels.

With its charming graphics and engaging gameplay, Zoo Boom is perfect for players of all ages looking for a fun way to pass the time and exercise their brain.

How to Play

  • Match three or more identical animals to remove them from the board
  • Create special combos by matching four or more animals
  • Complete level objectives before running out of moves
  • Unlock special power-ups and boosters as you progress
  • Challenge yourself with increasingly difficult puzzles
